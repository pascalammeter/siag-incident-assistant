# Milestones — SIAG Incident Management Assistent

## v1.0: MVP Launch

**Status:** ✅ Shipped 2026-04-06

**What shipped:**
- Complete 7-screen ransomware incident response wizard
- 25-point playbook with 4 phase structure (Sofortmassnahmen, Eindämmung, Untersuchung, Kommunikation)
- Schweizer Meldepflicht compliance (ISG 24h, DSG, FINMA 24/72h)
- SIAG branding and styling
- Mobile-responsive design (375px–2560px)
- Print-to-PDF export
- localStorage persistence
- 74/74 tests passing

**Production URL:** https://siag-incident-assistant.vercel.app

**Known limitations:**
- Single incident type (Ransomware only)
- No backend/database
- No authentication
- No multi-tenant support
